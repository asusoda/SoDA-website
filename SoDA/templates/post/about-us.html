About Us

Mission Statement

To serve and represent students and community in topics relating to technology, software, computing, and electronic communication. We foster collaboration and community among software developers of varying skills, experience, and specializations by providing a forum for the dissemination of relevant information and to thereby help students and attendees in software development. We will supplement curriculum by discussing any and all topics related to CS, Software and web development, programming, security, innovative technologies, and relevant mathematics. We will promote open and other standards on and off campus and to promote free choice of software for university-related tasks such as fulfilling class requirements and communication.
